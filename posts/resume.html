About me

Currently, I'm an M.S. Candidate at the the Institute of Computing Technology(ICT)  of Chinese Academy of Sciences(CAS). Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science atUniversity of Science and Technology of China(USTC) in July 2010.

I am in the the Microprocessor Technology Research Center of ICT, and do some design and verification work on Loongson,  which is a family of general-purpose MIPS-compatible CPUs.

My research interests are Computer Architecture and Formal verification(especial Model Checking).And I am also very interested in Parallel Computing,especial doing reaseachs(design, verification etc.) in parallel computing hardware like Graphics Processing Unit(GPU).

Education
